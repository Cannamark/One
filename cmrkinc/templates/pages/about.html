Our Culture

We see the future evolving right before our eyes, this is best seen through the Oleo Life brand. Sourcing and providing discreet and custom packaging solutions for a wide array of never before seen infused products is what we do best. The nature of the powdered products promotes an on the go lifestyle. Whether the consumer is out on the town, in the office, or at a festival, all they will need is a bottle of water to enjoy one of our many powdered beverages.

Our Mission

Our mission at Cannamark Inc. is to provide processors with the ability to produce innovative, safe, high margin products to supply emerging food manufacturers around the globe.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate people whose goal is to improve edible product manufacturing in ways that help the consumer enjoy safe, effective, and quality products. Our subsidiaries manufacture infused powder using our patent pending infusion process in tangent with our proprietary bio-polymer. This infused powder is in turn sold to edible manufacturers. Our infused powder products are designed to help maximize your company's potential while minimizing cost to the consumer.

Corporate Overview

Cannamark Inc. works to supply the food manufacturing industry with cutting edge products designed with consistency and safety in mind. Our wide array of products spans from automated vaporizer cartridge filling to industrial facility construction. Our patent pending water soluble powders not only are the way of the future, they are the best way for an new business to become a household name. In addition to our breakthrough encapsulation methods we offer a wide array of powdered products for processors to infuse. Our methodology allows for very efficient final goods processing. We aim to provide custom labeling and packaging solutions to our customers who must meet a wide array of regulatory requirements.
