The Oleo Binder
Our flagship product – Oleo Binder – is a proprietary, food safe biopolymer. This biopolymer, in conjunction with the patent pending Oleo Process, allows for efficient micro-encapsulation of cannabinoids. Our process creates a very fine (10-micron avg.), flavor neutral, cold water-soluble powder. We partner with manufacturers to provide Oleo Binder and specialized equipment required to conduct the Oleo Process on their cannabinoid extracts. The resulting infused powder enables scalable production and streamlined product development.

Using our infused powder to manufacture edibles is cost effective, efficient, and provides consistent, repeatable dosing with increased Bio-Availability.

Ponder the following scenario. Imagine you want to bake infused brownies: the extract you need to add to the brownies has a consistency similar to tree-sap; could you mix in tree-sap with the dry ingredients of your brownies easily? Conventional wisdom would have edible manufacturers dissolve the extract into a carrier oil. Edible manufacturers using that method have no option other than to tediously dose products to achieve consistency and quality.

Our powder streamlines your production.

Our water-soluble powder unlocks new product types and improves efficiency of your cannabinoids.

Our powders are cold water-soluble and shelf stable. This allows manufacturers to readily create powdered drink mixes, dietary supplement powders, and pressed tablets.

Water-soluble cannabinoids are also more bioavailable than their non-soluble counterparts.

Our infused powder has an average particle size of 10 microns and is cold water-soluble.

Bioavailability is how much of a compound your body can make use of at a given dose. Conventional edible cannabinoids have a bioavailability of about 20%, so if you consume 100mg your body can only process 20mg of the total. There are two main factors to focus on when trying to increase the bioavailability of something; water-solubility the size of each particle. Our infused powders have an average particle size of 10 microns, which is smaller than the average white blood cell at 25 microns.

Together, the high solubility and tiny particle size of our powders mean consumers feel the effects faster and stronger compared to other edibles.

Powder allows for superior dosage control.

Imagine that tree-sap extract once again. If you wanted to weigh out exactly 1mg of the tree-sap on a scale do you think you could do it? How long would it take if you could? Trying to add or remove 1mg of anything can be difficult, and nearly impossible with something that sticks to itself like tree-sap.
Now imagine you want to weigh out 1mg of our infused powder. You’d be able to remove 1 tiny grain of powder at a time, and you’d know exactly what the dosing will be if you remove that grain.

Using our infused powder you now only need a staff skilled enough to work in a kitchen.

Conventional methods of manufacturing edibles can be a big liability due to the necessity of having multiple food scientists and chemists on staff. That makes sense if your staff is handling complex mixing and measuring of extracts.

Using our infused powder you only need commercial kitchen staff and a single trained machinery operator – this can substantially reduce overhead.
